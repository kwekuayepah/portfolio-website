Quick Guidelines
Minimum Size
Don't go below 16px for the mark alone. For the horizontal lockup, minimum 32px mark height.
Clear Space
Maintain padding equal to at least 25% of the mark's width on all sides.
Typography
Use Source Serif 4 for headings and name. Inter for body text and UI elements.
Favicon
Use the mark alone with increased stroke width (8-14) at 16-32px sizes for clarity.
